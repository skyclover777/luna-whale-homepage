About the Artist

person

Elena Rivers

Master Folk Artist · 15+ years

Elena has been creating traditional folk art for over 15 years, specializing in Eastern European painting techniques. She studied at the Art Institute of Moscow and has taught over 1,000 students worldwide through workshops and online courses.
