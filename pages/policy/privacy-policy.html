Privacy Policy
Effective Date: March 1, 2025

1. Overview

Luna Whale Inc. ("Company," "we," "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use the Luna Whale Art Lab platform ("Service"). We comply with applicable data protection laws including the Korean Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

Category Items Collected Purpose
Registration Name, email, password, profile image (optional) Account creation & management
Payment Billing name, payment method, transaction history Subscription billing & refunds
Usage Classes viewed, lessons completed, gallery uploads, preferences Service improvement & personalization
Device IP address, browser type, OS, device identifiers Security, analytics & troubleshooting
Children Name, age, uploaded artwork (parent/guardian consent required) Class enrollment & gallery features

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you when you register, update your profile, or make a purchase
  • Automatically through cookies and analytics tools when you use the Service
  • From third-party payment processors for transaction verification
  • From educators or parents who register student accounts on behalf of minors

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service, including class access, curriculum delivery, and gallery features
  • To process payments, manage subscriptions, and handle refund requests
  • To personalize your experience with recommended classes and content
  • To send notifications, announcements, and educational updates (with your consent)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our educational offerings
  • To ensure platform security and prevent unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

5. Information Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Payment Processors: Secure third-party payment services to process your transactions (e.g., Stripe, Toss)
  • Cloud Services: Hosting and infrastructure providers for data storage and service delivery
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data with analytics services to improve the platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process

6. Children's Privacy

Luna Whale Art Lab serves children as part of our art education mission. For users under 14 years of age, we require verifiable parental or guardian consent before collecting personal information. Parents/guardians may review, modify, or delete their child's information at any time by contacting us. We minimize data collection for minors to only what is necessary for class participation and gallery features.

7. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 1 year after account deletion
  • Payment Records: Retained for 5 years as required by tax and commercial law
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 6 months for service improvement, then anonymized
  • User Content (Artwork): Retained until you delete it or request deletion. Backup copies are removed within 30 days

8. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including: encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest, secure password hashing, regular security audits, access controls limiting employee access to personal data, and automated threat monitoring. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, visit the Privacy Settings page or contact us at privacy@lunawhale.com.

10. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for essential functions (login sessions, language preferences, dark mode setting), analytics (anonymous usage statistics), and personalization (recommended content). You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect Service functionality.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. When transferring data internationally, we use appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and ensure that receiving parties provide adequate protection in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or email at least 7 days before taking effect. The "Effective Date" at the top indicates the most recent revision. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
