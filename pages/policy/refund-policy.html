Refund Policy
Effective Date: March 1, 2025

At Luna Whale Art Lab, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you find that our materials do not meet your expectations, we offer a straightforward refund policy. For individual lesson purchases, you may request a full refund within 7 days of purchase, provided the content has not been downloaded or accessed. Premium Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time through your account settings, with access remaining until the end of the current billing cycle. Annual plans may be eligible for a pro-rated refund within the first 14 days of the term. To initiate a refund request, please contact our support team at support@lunawhale.com with your order details.
